How it works

From "now what?"
to a clear plan.

Three minutes of your time. A personalized vision of your wedding, a realistic budget breakdown, and curated vendor recommendations — all in one place.

01

Tell us your vision

Share your location, date, guest count, budget, and style. Three quick steps covering everything we need to personalize your experience.

02

See your ceremony

An AI-generated ceremony scene appears — styled to match your venue type, colour palette, and aesthetic. Uniquely yours, not a stock photo.

Interactive budget callouts break your total into categories — Venue, Florals, Photography, Music, and more — based on real Vancouver pricing.

Curated recommendations matched to your vision and per-category budget. Save favourites, compare side-by-side, and plan with confidence.

Actual starting prices from every vendor before you reach out. No surprises, no bait-and-switch quotes, no chasing people for ballpark figures.
