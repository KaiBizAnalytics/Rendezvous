**Region:** Sea to Sky Corridor | **City:** Pemberton | **Neighbourhood:** Pemberton Valley
**Type:** Farm / Agricultural Venue | **Setting:** Outdoor / Mixed

> Working farm in the breathtaking Pemberton Valley at the foot of iconic Mount Currie. One of the most photographed and beloved wedding venues in the Sea to Sky — famous for stunning long-table field dinners with Mt. Currie as backdrop, and raucous barn dance parties.
