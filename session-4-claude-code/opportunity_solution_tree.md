# Opportunity Solution Tree

**Product:** AI Wedding Concierge (Rendezvous)

## Desired Outcome

Reduce wedding planning time and stress by **50%** by helping couples
quickly find realistic vendors, make decisions faster, and organize
planning in one place.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Opportunities (User Problems From Interviews)

## 1. Vendor Research Takes Too Long

Couples spend hours researching venues and vendors only to discover they
are unrealistic for their budget, style, or guest count.

**Key insight:** People want **5 relevant options**, not **100 generic
ones**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Lack of Transparent Pricing

Wedding vendors often hide pricing, forcing couples to contact many
vendors just to estimate budgets.

**Key insight:** Budget filtering is more valuable than inspiration
tools.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Fragmented Planning Tools

Planning currently happens across multiple disconnected tools like
Google Sheets, Pinterest, vendor websites, email threads, and social
media.

**Key insight:** Existing tools store information but do not help
couples make decisions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Decision Fatigue

Couples must make hundreds of small decisions, which becomes
overwhelming during planning.

**Key insight:** Users want decision support rather than long checklists
or inspiration galleries.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Cultural & Personalization Gaps

Existing wedding tools assume Western weddings and do not adapt well to
multicultural ceremonies or different wedding traditions.

**Key insight:** Personalization must include culture, budget, guest
count, and location.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Solutions (Product Ideas)

## AI Wedding Concierge

A conversational AI assistant that guides couples through planning, asks
questions about their wedding, and provides tailored recommendations.

Capabilities: - Budget-aware recommendations - Cultural customization -
Vendor discovery - Planning guidance

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Personalized Vendor Matching

Instead of browsing large vendor directories, couples receive **5--10
curated recommendations** that match:

-   Budget
-   Guest count
-   Wedding style
-   Cultural requirements
-   Location

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Transparent Pricing Engine

Aggregate pricing insights from vendors and real weddings.

Features: - Estimated price ranges - Budget planning tools - Vendor
price comparison - Real wedding cost benchmarks

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Wedding Planning Hub

A centralized platform combining:

-   Vendor shortlist
-   Budget tracker
-   Planning checklist
-   Timeline management
-   Notes and communication

This replaces the current fragmented workflow across multiple tools.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Cultural Wedding Planning Modules

Optional planning modules tailored to specific wedding traditions.

Examples: - Indian weddings (mehndi, sangeet, multi‑day events) -
Chinese weddings (tea ceremony, banquet) - Multicultural ceremonies -
LGBTQ‑friendly vendor discovery

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Assumption Tests (Experiments)

## Test 1: Recommendation Value

Show users 5 personalized vendor recommendations and measure if they
prefer this over browsing vendor directories.

## Test 2: Pricing Transparency

Prototype a price‑filtered venue search and measure whether it reduces
research time.

## Test 3: Concierge Interaction

Test an AI chat interface for planning questions and evaluate whether
couples trust AI guidance.

## Test 4: Cultural Personalization

Prototype a culturally‑aware onboarding questionnaire and test whether
recommendations feel relevant.
